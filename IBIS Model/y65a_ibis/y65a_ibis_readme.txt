Y65A IBIS Model 
Die Rev L
128Mb SDRAM IBIS model
Rev 1.0: 12/13/2010
Rev 2.0: 08/25/2011
Rev 2.1: 09/03/2019


y65a.ibs is applicable to Commercial temperature range (0C to 70C)
y65a_it.ibs is applicable to Industrial temperature range (-40C to 85C)
y65a_at.ibs is applicable to Automotive temperature range (-40C to 105C)

Model is valid for 100, 133, 143, 167 MHz.

Model data is extracted from the HSPICE model, revision 2.0.
